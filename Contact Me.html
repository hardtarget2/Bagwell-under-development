Booking Information

Below you'll find my booking information. please read before contacting.

  1. A $30 fee is required to be paid to secure your spot, the $30 does not go toward your Service
  2. I only Accept Payments in Cash, PayPal, Apple Pay, Navy Federal or Cash App
  3. If you are more than 15 minutes late, you will be charged a $15 late fee
  4. If you are 25 minutes late, your appointment will be canceled. 100% of your service will be charged from the card on file. if your card declines it must be paid before re-booking your next appointment
  5. Please reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment
  6. If you cancel less than 24 hours of your appointment, a $25 fee will be added to your next appointment
  7. No call/no show will be charged 100% of your service from the car on file. No show twice in a row will be unable to schedule any further appointments and all future appointments will be canceled

I Look forward to doing business with you!
